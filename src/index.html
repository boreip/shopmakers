Why Shopify

Secure & Reliable

Shopify takes security seriously. It implements strong access measures, protects cardholder data and maintains a vulnerability managment program. All stores powered by Shopify are Level 1 PCI DSS compliant.

Affordable

Shopify offers several plans and price options to choose from. Compared to self-hosted shops, it has an incredible price-to-quality ratio. Even the cheapest plan includes essential features and the App Store includes thousands of free apps.

Performant & Scalable

Shopify can equip your site to easily handle high traffic volumes and sales spikes. Shopify cloud-based infrastructure is designed for resiliency, with servers capable of handling thousands of transactions per minute. The result is 99.99% uptime.
